Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the purpose of this logbook system?
The logbook system helps students track and record their daily academic activities, providing a structured way to document progress weekly.
2. How do I use the logbook system?
Navigate to the week, click the day, fill in the details, and save your entry. Completed days will show a green check icon.
3. How do I know which days I’ve already filled in?
Completed days are marked with a green check icon (`✓`) beside the day button.
4. Can I edit my entries after saving?
Yes. Click on the completed day to load the saved data into the form and make changes. Save the updated entry when done.
5. What happens to my data after I save it?
Your data is stored locally on your device, ensuring it is accessible even after refreshing the page. However, it won’t sync across devices.
6. Can I access my logbook on a different device?
Not yet. Data is stored locally. Future updates may include cloud synchronization for multi-device access.
7. What should I do if I accidentally delete my entries?
If local storage is cleared, data cannot be recovered. Double-check before clearing your browser storage or cache.
8. Who should I contact if I face issues with the system?
Reach out to your program administrator or the IT support team for help.
